## Overview

HypeDocs is an AI-powered audio journaling app designed to empower users by analyzing spoken entries and extracting key accomplishments. This process helps users reflect on their positives, fostering confidence and well-being.

## Key Features

- **AI-Powered Transcription**: Converts spoken words into text seamlessly.
- **Accomplishment Extraction**: Analyzes audio entries to identify significant achievements.
- **Intuitive Journaling Tools**: User-friendly interface for efficient journaling.
