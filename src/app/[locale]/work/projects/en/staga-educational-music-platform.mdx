## Overview

Staga is an innovative app designed to assist schools in teaching music effectively. As the Technical Advisor and Developer, I helped build the framework using Bubble, contributing to its core infrastructure and interactive features.

## Key Features

- **Course Progression System**: A structured system for teachers and students to navigate lessons.
- **ToneJS Integration**: Enhanced the app's music capabilities.
- **Custom Plugin Development**: Allowed students to interact with musical components directly.

## Collaborators

Worked with the founders, designers, product team, and other developers.

## Status

The app has been launched and is currently in early testing, receiving positive feedback from initial users.
